Good To Know

Membership FAQs

Can I pause my membership? +
Yes, every plan allows one free pause of up to 30 days per year — useful for travel, injury, or exams.
Is there a joining fee? +
No joining fee for Starter and Performance. Elite includes a one-time onboarding assessment.
Can I switch plans later? +
Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your next billing cycle with no penalty.
Do you offer student discounts? +
Yes, students get 15% off the Starter and Performance plans with a valid student card.
