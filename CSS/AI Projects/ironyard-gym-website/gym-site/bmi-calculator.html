Understanding the BMI ranges.

BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnosis — muscle mass can skew results.

U

Underweight

Below 18.5 — may indicate insufficient energy intake for your activity level.

N

Normal

18.5 – 24.9 — generally associated with lower risk of weight-related conditions.

O

Overweight

25.0 – 29.9 — a good point to reassess training volume and nutrition habits.

B

Obese

30.0 and above — worth a conversation with a coach and a structured plan.
