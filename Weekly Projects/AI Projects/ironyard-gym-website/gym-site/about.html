IronYard started as a single free-weight room in 2019. Today it's Bahawalpur's home for strength training, conditioning, and honest coaching.

Our Story

Strength training, done properly.

IronYard opened with six barbells and a simple idea: most people don't need a fancier gym, they need better coaching. That idea hasn't changed even as the floor has grown to 8,000 sq ft of free weights, machines, and conditioning space.

We still believe the fundamentals — squat, hinge, press, pull, carry — beat any trend. Every program we write comes back to those basics, taught properly and progressed patiently.

Timeline

How we got here.

2019

The First Floor

Opened a single-room free-weight gym in Model Town with three founding coaches and forty members.

2021

Conditioning Zone Added

Expanded into the adjacent unit to add a dedicated HIIT and cardio conditioning space.

2023

Nutrition Coaching Launched

Brought on a licensed dietitian to offer meal planning alongside every Elite membership.

2026

1,200+ Members Strong

Now Bahawalpur's largest strength-focused gym, with 14 certified coaches across five specialties.
