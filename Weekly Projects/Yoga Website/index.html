OUR STORY

Started with a mat, a park bench, and a few curious neighbours.

Shafiq began practicing yoga alone in the early morning quiet of his local park. A few regulars noticed, asked to join, and within months a loose circle of neighbours had turned into a standing routine — green shirts, shared mats, and a WhatsApp group that never really goes quiet.

There's no studio, no membership desk. Just a patch of grass, an instructor who still practices like a student, and a community that shows up for each other as much as for the poses.
